Who We Are

About Luak Holdings

Luak Holdings Limited is a Ugandan multi-sector holding company built on a foundation of professional excellence, integrity, and a relentless commitment to delivering tangible results for every client we partner with.

Founded and headquartered in Uganda, Luak Holdings was born from a clear vision: to be the partner that businesses, institutions, and individuals turn to when they need trusted expertise across the full spectrum of their growth journey.

We operate across six strategically chosen sectors — Technology, Property, Surveying, Finance, Logistics, and Supply Chain — giving our clients access to a complete ecosystem of professional services under one roof.

Our team is composed of seasoned professionals who bring deep sector knowledge, a hands-on work ethic, and a genuine passion for impact. At Luak Holdings, we are not merely service providers — we are growth partners, committed for the long term.

Our Vision

To be Uganda's foremost multi-sector holding company — recognised for trusted expertise, lasting client relationships, and measurable value creation across every industry we serve.

Our Mission

To deliver integrated, professional services that empower businesses and individuals to grow with confidence — combining sector expertise, personal integrity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
